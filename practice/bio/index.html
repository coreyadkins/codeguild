John Cena Bio

John Felix Anthony Cena Jr. (born April 23, 1977) is an American professional wrestler, rapper, actor, and reality television show host signed to WWE on the SmackDown brand.

Places lived in

John Cena was born in West Newbury, Massachusetts. He currently resides in Tampa, Florida.
